How We Started


At PlaceME.com, we are passionate about helping job seekers find their dream jobs and employers find the perfect candidates for their business. But how did we get started?


It all began when our founder, Amar, was looking for a job himself. He found the job search process to be overwhelming, time-consuming, and frustrating. He spent hours browsing through job boards, sending out resumes and cover letters, and waiting for responses. Even when he did get a response, it was often a generic rejection letter that left him feeling discouraged and rejected.


That's when John realized that there had to be a better way. He started doing research and talking to other job seekers and employers to understand their pain points and needs. He discovered that many job seekers and employers were experiencing the same challenges that he had faced.


Driven by his passion to make a difference, Amar decided to create PlaceME.com, a platform that would make the job search process easier, faster, and more effective for both job seekers and employers.


Our mission is to empower job seekers with the tools and resources they need to find their dream jobs and employers with the best talent for their businesses. We strive to create a seamless and user-friendly platform that connects job seekers with potential employers and provides employers with the tools they need to find the right candidates.


We are constantly innovating and improving our platform to meet the evolving needs of the job market. We are committed to delivering the highest level of service and support to our users and making a positive impact in the lives of job seekers and employers.


Thank you for choosing PlaceME.com as your job search partner. We are excited to be a part of your journey towards career success!
